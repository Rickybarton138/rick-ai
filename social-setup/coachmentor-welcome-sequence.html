Day 2
Quick-start guide ✦
Plan your best-ever session in 5 minutes flat
Hi [First Name],
Most coaches open CoachMentor for the first time and spend 20 minutes just exploring. That's fine — but I want to make sure you actually use it at your next training session, not just have a look around.
Here's the exact 5-minute path to a full session plan:
1
Tell CoachMentor your context (30 seconds)
Open the app → tap "Plan a Session" → enter your age group, how many players you'll have, and session length. That's it.
2
Pick your session objective (1 minute)
Pressing? Possession? Transitions? Defending? Choose one. If you don't know, use the AI Mentor: "What should I work on with my U11s right now?"
3
Get your full plan (instant)
CoachMentor generates a warm-up, main activity, drill with progressions, and a cool-down coaching question. Tap any drill to see the full diagram and setup.
4
Save it and go coach (30 seconds)
Tap "Save Session". It's in your library forever. Screenshot the drill diagram to have on your phone at the pitch. Done.
This week's drill to try
⚡ Quick Win — Rondo with Pressure Triggers
AgeU10 upward
Time12 minutes
Setup5v2 rondo in a 12×12 grid. Outside players keep possession. Middle two press on a signal.
Key Q."What's the signal that tells us when to press? What changes in the body shape of the player on the ball?"
ProgressShrink the grid. Add a 3rd defender. Give outside players a 5-second possession limit.
