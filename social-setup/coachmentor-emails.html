⚡ This Week's Drill — 3v2 Pressing Trigger
Age U9 and above
Time 15–20 minutes
Players 9+ (split into groups of 5)
Setup 20x15 yard grid. 3 attackers vs 2 defenders. Server outside grid with 3 spare balls.
Task Defenders must press as a pair — trigger = server's body opens to play. First defender pressures, second covers. Attackers score in small goal if they survive 8 seconds.
Key Q. "When is the right moment to press? What tells you it's time to go?"
Progress Add a third defender. Reduce time to 6 seconds. Allow defenders to counter-attack after a win.

Inside CoachMentor, every drill has a full diagram, a video prompt guide, and a linked coaching question to use with your players. This is just the text version.
